Cebu City Job Vacancies



Elevator Maintenance
Job Description:

Handles the maintenance, inspection, troubleshooting, and repair of elevators and escalators to ensure safety and efficiency. Duties include checking mechanical and electrical components, responding to service calls, conducting preventive maintenance, and submitting service reports. Coordinates with building management and escalates complex issues when necessary.

Qualifications:

Applicants should have knowledge and experience in electrical or mechanical systems, ideally with hands-on work in elevator or escalator maintenance. A vocational or technical course in Electrical Technology, Mechatronics, or a related field is preferred. Must be physically fit, detail-oriented, and capable of working at heights or in confined spaces. Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently or in a team are required. TESDA NC II or NC III in Elevator Installation and Maintenance (or equivalent) is highly preferred.

Painter
Job Description:

Monitors and maintains the cleanliness and safety of the swimming pool area. Assists guests, provides pool towels, checks chemical levels, and ensures all safety protocols are followed.

Qualifications:

Applicants must have experience in residential, commercial, or industrial painting. Must be knowledgeable in proper surface preparation, color mixing, and application techniques. A keen eye for detail, physical stamina, and the ability to follow instructions and safety guidelines are essential. Candidates who have worked in construction or building maintenance are preferred. TESDA certification or equivalent training in painting or building maintenance is an advantage but not required.

Audio-Visual (AV) Technician
Job Description:

Responsible for setting up, operating, and maintaining audio-visual equipment for events, presentations, or facility use. This includes handling sound systems, microphones, projectors, screens, lighting, and control systems. Duties involve system checks, troubleshooting, and ensuring all equipment functions properly before, during, and after use, while coordinating with clients or event teams.

Qualifications:

Applicants should have a good understanding of audio, video, and lighting equipment, with preferred experience in event, hospitality, or corporate AV setups. Must be detail-oriented, technically skilled, and able to perform under time pressure. A vocational or technical course in Electronics, IT, or a related field is an advantage. S trong communication and time management skills are essential, along with the flexibility to work shifting schedules or extended hours.

Public Attendant
Job Description:

The Public Area Attendant is responsible for maintaining the cleanliness, sanitation, and overall appearance of all common areas such as lobbies, hallways, restrooms, elevators, stairways, and outdoor spaces. Duties include sweeping, mopping, dusting, disinfecting, collecting trash, restocking supplies, and reporting any maintenance issues. The attendant ensures that all public areas meet cleanliness standards at all times.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be physically fit and capable of performing manual cleaning tasks throughout the shift. Must be detail-oriented, trustworthy, and able to follow cleaning procedures and safety protocols. Prior experience in housekeeping, janitorial work, or public area maintenance is an advantage but not required. Willingness to work in shifts, including weekends and holidays, is expected. Good attitude and professionalism in interacting with guests and co-workers are also important.

Electromechanical Technician
Job Description:

The Electromechanical Technician is responsible for the installation, maintenance, and repair of machines and systems that involve both electrical and mechanical components. This includes troubleshooting motors, control systems, automation equipment, sensors, and mechanical parts. The technician ensures that all equipment operates efficiently, safely, and in compliance with standard operating procedures and safety regulations. Tasks may include routine inspections, calibration, and responding to equipment breakdowns.

Qualifications:

Candidates must have a strong background in both electrical and mechanical systems, preferably with experience in manufacturing, industrial, or building maintenance settings. Completion of a vocational or technical course in Electromechanics, Mechatronics, or related fields is required. Must be able to read technical diagrams, use diagnostic tools, and perform hands-on repairs independently. TESDA certification in Electromechanical Technology or equivalent is a strong advantage. Good problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and a willingness to work in shifts or extended hours when needed are essential.

Aircon Technician
Job Description:

The Aircon Technician is responsible for the installation, maintenance, and repair of air conditioning systems in residential, commercial, or industrial settings. This includes cleaning units, checking electrical connections, troubleshooting mechanical and refrigerant issues, performing preventive maintenance, and ensuring all air conditioning systems are functioning safely and efficiently. The technician may also handle split-type, window-type, or centralized AC systems depending on the client’s requirements.

Qualifications:

Applicants must have a solid understanding of air conditioning systems, electrical wiring, and basic refrigeration principles. Completion of a vocational or technical course in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning or a related field is required. TESDA certification (RAC Servicing NC II) is a strong advantage. Must be physically fit, detail-oriented, and capable of working independently or as part of a team. Prior experience in AC installation and maintenance is preferred. Willingness to work on-call, during weekends, or in shifting schedules is also expected.

BMS Technician
Job Description:

The BMS Technician is responsible for the operation, maintenance, and troubleshooting of the Building Management System that controls and monitors mechanical and electrical equipment such as HVAC, lighting, fire alarms, energy systems, and elevators. The technician ensures all systems are functioning efficiently and securely, performs system checks, conducts routine inspections, responds to alarms or faults, and coordinates with other maintenance personnel to ensure building safety and energy efficiency.

Qualifications:

Applicants must have a solid background in electrical, electronics, or mechanical systems with specific knowledge in BMS operations and controls. A diploma or technical course in Electronics, Electrical Technology, or Mechatronics is preferred. Prior experience in BMS or facilities management is a strong advantage. Candidates must be computer-literate, detail-oriented, and capable of reading electrical diagrams and operating BMS software. TESDA certifications or relevant system training (e.g., Schneider, Siemens, Honeywell) are highly desirable. Willingness to work on shifting schedules and respond to emergency issues is expected.
