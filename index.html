Frequently Asked Questions

Romac Visayas Services Inc. specializes in providing end-to-end manpower solutions to businesses across various industries. Our services include recruitment and staffing, on-site HR administration, employee training, and payroll processing. We cater to companies in retail, logistics, hospitality, manufacturing, and construction, ensuring that every client gets skilled, qualified, and reliable personnel to meet their operational needs.

We understand that time is crucial for your business operations. That’s why we’ve designed our hiring and deployment process to be fast and efficient. Once we receive your job requirements, our recruitment team immediately begins candidate screening and matching. Depending on the volume and nature of the job order, we can deploy qualified candidates within a few days, ensuring minimal disruption to your business.

Yes, Romac Visayas Services Inc. is fully accredited and compliant with all necessary government regulations, including the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE), Social Security System (SSS), Pag-IBIG, and PhilHealth. Our compliance ensures that both our clients and our deployed personnel are protected by labor laws and receive the appropriate benefits and legal safeguards.

Yes, we welcome walk-in applicants and online submissions. Job seekers may apply through our official website, send their resume via email, or visit any of our branch offices. We also regularly post job openings on our official Facebook page. Our recruitment team conducts interviews, screens applicants, and matches them with suitable job opportunities based on their qualifications and preferences.

Romac Visayas Services Inc. has several strategically located branches across the Visayas region, including Cebu City, Iloilo City, Bacolod City, Boracay and other key provinces. These branches allow us to be more accessible to job seekers and responsive to the staffing needs of our clients. Each branch is managed by a dedicated team that ensures quality service and smooth coordination for both recruitment and deployment.
