Boracay Job Vacancies



Housekeeping
Job Description:

Responsible for maintaining cleanliness and orderliness in guest rooms, hallways, and public areas. Performs routine cleaning tasks such as sweeping, mopping, making beds, restocking amenities, and reporting maintenance issues.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be physically fit, detail-oriented, and capable of performing repetitive tasks. Prior housekeeping experience is an advantage but not required. Willingness to work flexible hours and maintain high cleanliness standards is essential.

Pool Attendant
Job Description:

Monitors and maintains the cleanliness and safety of the swimming pool area. Assists guests, provides pool towels, checks chemical levels, and ensures all safety protocols are followed.

Qualifications:

Must be attentive, responsible, and able to work under the sun for extended periods. Experience in pool maintenance or lifeguard training is preferred. Good communication skills and customer service orientation are required.

Technician
Job Description:

Handles basic repairs and maintenance for electrical, plumbing, air-conditioning, and other facility systems. Ensures all equipment is functioning properly and safely.

Qualifications:

Candidates must have basic knowledge of electrical and mechanical systems. Experience in facility maintenance or related technical work is required. Must be able to troubleshoot and perform repairs with minimal supervision.

Room Attendant
Job Description:

Provides cleaning and maintenance for guest rooms, including making beds, changing linens, cleaning bathrooms, vacuuming, and replenishing supplies to ensure a clean and welcoming environment.

Qualifications:

Must be hardworking, reliable, and able to follow cleaning procedures efficiently. Prior experience as a room attendant is a plus but not mandatory. Physical stamina and attention to detail are essential.

Front Office
Job Description:

Serves as the first point of contact for guests, handles check-ins and check-outs, answers inquiries, and provides excellent customer service at the reception area.

Qualifications:

Applicants must have strong communication and interpersonal skills, a professional appearance, and good English proficiency. Experience in customer service or front desk work is preferred. Basic computer literacy is required.

Driver
Job Description:

Responsible for the safe and timely transportation of staff, guests, or goods. Ensures proper maintenance and cleanliness of the assigned vehicle.

Qualifications:

Candidates must have a valid driver’s license and a clean driving record. Must be familiar with local routes and traffic laws. Punctuality, reliability, and basic knowledge of vehicle troubleshooting are required.

Kitchen Helper
Job Description:

Assists in food preparation, washing dishes, cleaning kitchen areas, and performing other tasks assigned by kitchen staff to ensure efficient operations.

Qualifications:

Must be physically fit, willing to learn, and capable of working in a fast-paced kitchen environment. Experience in food handling or kitchen work is an advantage. Must be flexible and team-oriented.

Pastry
Job Description:

Prepares and bakes pastries, desserts, and bread. Ensures consistency in taste and presentation while maintaining cleanliness and food safety in the pastry section.

Qualifications:

Applicants must have knowledge in pastry preparation and baking techniques. Experience in a pastry or bakery setting is highly preferred. Creativity, precision, and time management are important skills for this role.

Staff House Crew
Job Description:

Maintains the cleanliness and orderliness of the staff house including common areas, rooms, and facilities. Assists in basic upkeep and coordinates with management for any concerns.

Qualifications:

Must be responsible, trustworthy, and capable of performing housekeeping duties. Willing to work with minimal supervision and maintain a clean and organized living environment for staff.

Staff House Kantix
Job Description:

Prepares and serves food in the staff house, maintains cleanliness in the kitchen and dining area, and ensures that meals are properly portioned and served on time.

Qualifications:

Must be familiar with basic kitchen operations and food handling. Experience in canteen or food service work is an advantage. Must be neat, organized, and able to work independently or as part of a small team.

Carpenter
Job Description:

Responsible for constructing, repairing, and installing wooden structures such as doors, walls, and furniture. Must be able to interpret blueprints, take accurate measurements, and operate hand and power tools

Qualifications:

Applicants should be at least high school graduates with 1–2 years of carpentry experience. Ability to read drawings is required, and TESDA certification is an advantage.

Plumber
Job Description:

Installs and repairs piping systems, water lines, and plumbing fixtures such as sinks, toilets, and faucets. Must be able to read blueprints and follow building codes and safety regulations.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be at least high school graduates with relevant plumbing experience. TESDA NC II in Plumbing is preferred.
