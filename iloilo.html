IloIlo City Job Vacancies



Janitor/Janitress
Job Description:

Maintaining the cleanliness and oderliness of building.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be at least high school level. Preferably with previous experience, but willing-to-train candidates are welcome. Open to both male and female applicants.

Electricians
Job Description:

Responsible for installing, repairing, and maintaining electrical systems, wiring and fixtures in residential, commercial, or industrial buildings

Qualifications:

Applicants must be at least a high school or senior high school graduate. Possession of a TESDA NC II certification in Electrical Installation and Maintenance is preferred. Candidates with 1–2 years of relevant experience in residential, commercial, or industrial electrical work are ideal. required.

Carpenter
Job Description:

Responsible for constructing, repairing, and installing wooden structures such as doors, walls, and furniture. Must be able to interpret blueprints, take accurate measurements, and operate hand and power tools

Qualifications:

Applicants should be at least high school graduates with 1–2 years of carpentry experience. Ability to read drawings is required, and TESDA certification is an advantage.

Plumber
Job Description:

Installs and repairs piping systems, water lines, and plumbing fixtures such as sinks, toilets, and faucets. Must be able to read blueprints and follow building codes and safety regulations.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be at least high school graduates with relevant plumbing experience. TESDA NC II in Plumbing is preferred.

Foam Mattress Assembler (Male)
Job Description:

Responsible for cutting, shaping, and assembling foam materials into mattresses according to specifications. Uses tools and adhesives to ensure high-quality output.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be at least high school level, with or without prior experience. Willingness to learn and attention to detail are essential.

Chairmaker Upholsterer (Male)
Job Description:

Handles the installation of padding and coverings such as fabric or leather on chairs and furniture frames. Uses hand tools and sewing equipment to ensure a clean and smooth finish.

Qualifications:

Must be at least a high school graduate with experience in upholstery work. Precision and good hand-eye coordination are required.

Chairmaker Framer (Male)
Job Description:

Builds the wooden or metal frame structure for chairs and similar furniture using basic tools and machinery.

Qualifications:

Applicants should be at least high school level with basic carpentry skills and the ability to follow measurements accurately. Experience is preferred but not required.

Chairmaker Webber (Male)
Job Description:

Responsible for securing webbing or elastic supports under chairs and seats as part of the upholstery process. Must be physically fit and able to perform repetitive tasks.

Qualifications:

High school level education is acceptable. Experience is not required, but a willingness to learn is important.

Chairmaker Cutter (Male)
Job Description:

Cuts foam, fabric, leather, and other upholstery materials according to patterns and measurements. Uses manual or mechanical cutting tools to ensure accuracy.

Qualifications:

Applicants must be at least high school level, with or without experience. Familiarity with cutting tools is a plus.
